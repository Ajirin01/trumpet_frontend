HYMN 12 R.H. 372

KEYG ALL MUST BE WELL
  1. Through the Love of God our
    Saviour,
    All must be well!
    Free and changeless is His
    favour,
    All, all is well!
    Precious is the blood that
    healed us;
    Perfect is the grace that
    sealed us;
    Strong the hand stretched
    forth to shield us,
    All must be well!
  2. Though we pass through
    tribulations
    All must be well!
    Ours is such a full salvation,
    All, all is well!
    Happy still in God confiding
    Fruitful if in Christ abiding
    Holy through the spirit’s
    guiding;
    All must be well!
  3. We expect a bright tomorrow,
    All will be well!
    Faith can sing through days of
    sorrow
    All, all is well!
    On our father’s love relying
    Jesus every need supplying,
    Or in living or in dying,
    All must be well!!

A Speech delivered by the Head Prefect, Morenikeji Olayiwola Michael @ the 4th Graduation, Speech and Prize Giving of Trumpet Sound Academy, Minna

Held on 19th July, 2018.

The Board Chairman, The Head Teacher, Special Guest of Honour, The Chairman, Parent Staff Fellowship, Our ever Hard Working Teachers, Our Loving Parents, The Graduating Pupils, Ladies and Gentlemen,

It is my pleasure to present this speech on behalf of the graduating pupils. Today marks a milestone in our academic life. The little steps that we have been taking since we entered this special school have now become a giant leap. We have just completed a phase in our academic journeys and we give glory and honour to God Almighty. We are different because this school is also different in terms of what we are taught, how we are taught and the discipline instilled in us. For this we say thank you to our hard working supervisors. We thank our parents also for believing in the school and choosing to send us to this school. In conclusion, I want to declare boldly that we have been well prepared for further education anywhere in the world and we promise that we will not disappoint you by the grace of God. Thank you, God bless Trumpet Sound Academy, God Bless Nigeria.
